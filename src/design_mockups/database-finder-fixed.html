ABA/BNA Lawyers' Manual on Professional ConductThis link opens in a new window
Analysis, guidance, primary source material and news and developments on attorney conduct and legal ethics. Includes full text of ABA ethics opinions issued from 1986 onward, digests of state and local ethics opinions from 1986 to the present, links to state Web sites for full text of state ethics rules and opinions, practice guides by topics of how laws have been applied, and reports on court decisions, ethics opinions, disciplinary actions, and other news from 1998 to the present.
ABELL: Annual Bibliography of English LiteratureThis link opens in a new window
Alternative Name(s):: ProQuest One Literature
Covers monographs, periodical articles, critical editions of literary works, book reviews, collections of essays and doctoral dissertations on English language and literatures. (Coverage: 1920-present). Now part of ProQuest One Literature. To search ABELL alone, click on "Criticism" and then select ABELL on the left-hand sidebar.
ABIA - Index of South and Southeast Asian Art and ArchaeologyThis link opens in a new window
This bibliography is dedicated to South and Southeast Asian prehistory, archaeology of the historical period, art, crafts and architecture (from early down to contemporary), inscriptions and palaeography, coins and seals of these regions.
